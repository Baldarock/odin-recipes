Chana Masala Recipe

A bowl of the delicious vegan curry, chana masala.

Chana masala can be made with chickpeas, tomatoes, and a variety of indian spices.

Chana masala is a naturally vegan dish, meaning that no animal products are used in the creation of the dish.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Gharam Masala
  • Ginger Garlic Paste

Directions

  1. Wash chickpeas and cook until soft.
  2. Chop onions and tomatoes to small pieces.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of ginger garlic paste to bowl of onions and tomatoes.
  4. Cook mixture on stove on high for 20 minutes.
  5. Add chickpeas and gharam masala at the end, add gharam masala to taste.
