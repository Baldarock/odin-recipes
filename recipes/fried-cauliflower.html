Fried Cauliflower Recipe

Fried cauliflower can be a delicious appetizer or snack!

Fried cauliflower is an easy to prepare meal with a small list of ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Corn Starch

Directions

  1. Wash cauliflower and break into bite-size pieces.
  2. Cover cauliflower in flour.
  3. Toss in the baking powder, baking soda, and corn starch into a bowl.
  4. Toss the cauliflower in the mixture.
  5. Bake the cauliflower at 425 degrees for 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
